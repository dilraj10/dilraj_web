Early Years

The Lakers franhise began in 1947 when (with the help of Sid Hartman) Ben Berger ad Morris Chalfen purchased the disbanded Detroit Gems for $15000. The name Lakers was inspired by nickname,"land of 10000 lakes" and became known as the Minnesota Lakers. The team made the switch from Minnesota to Los Angelos in 1960. After the switch is when the intense rivalry between the celtics and the lakers began.

Success

The Los Angeles Lakers are the second most decorated and successful team in NBA history. They have won 16 Championships which is only 1 less then the Boston Celtics. The Lakers also have an astonishing 31 conference titles which means that they are 16 for 31 when it comes to the finals. The Lakers have won their division 23 times aswell. The Lakers have had many great players on their team and have 11 retired jerseys of some of the worlds best players. The Lakers franchise hold many records in the National Basketball Association. These include: most consecutive wins(33), most consecutive road wins(16) most finals appearances(31), the largest margin victory(63 points), ad many more. There is no question that the Lakers are one of the most illustrious and successful teams in the NBA.
